The Project

Give Glucovation, a realtime blood glucose monitoring product, a rebrand and application.

Realtime blood glucose monitoring

The Solution

Redesign Glucovation's product ecosystem and identity. Then, introduce Vis, Glucovation's new sub-brand, into the sporting world. Putting this product through trial by the most demanding athletes will elevate the credibility and accelerate the adoption into a critical mass of users.

The Role

We explored mobile implementations for Vis then I redesign the UI making it more comprehensive while maintaining simplicty and function. We concepted OOH digital experiences which I mocked-up.

XD Thinking

While desinging a complete mobile experience for individuals we also understand that additional markets have unique needs we need to tailor to. Our interfaces have features specific to the needs that will be needed by the respective user. The event installations give people new to Vis the opportunity to see it in action and interact with the UI over a large touch screen. The initial commercial application suggested is sports, but it can be extended into many industry not limited to wellness, military, etc.
