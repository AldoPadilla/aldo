    The Project

    Manifest the Mountain Hardwear brand into a product that will supplement the brand's current products.

    Preserve Experiences

    The Solution

    Preventing the loss of Mountain Hardwear (MH) gear could potentially stand in the way of a life threatening situation, but it is certain that it will definitely keep you from the disappointment of losing your gear.
    Mountain Hardwear products are designed with an expertise trusted in the most extreme outdoor endeavors. While many MH product users do not put their gear through these trials, MH continues to maintain this level of quality throughout their products.

    The Role

    My role spanned from strategy, product design, arduino coding, copywriting, filming, and editing.
