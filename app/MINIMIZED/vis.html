    The Project

    Design a campaign project around a real time blood glucose monitor. Brand the product and suggest applications for use and exposure.

    Real-time blood glucose monitoring

    The Solution

    Design a product ecosystem and identity. Introduce Vis into the sporting world. Putting this product through trial by the most demanding athletes will elevate the credibility and accelerate the adoption into a critical mass of users.

    The Role

    My primary role was to concept, design for the digital & physical executions, and suggest tech solutions for alternative applications of Vis. Together with Mo and James we concepted the executables for the campaign. We explored mobile implementations for Vis then I redesigned the UIs making them more comprehensive while maintaining simplicity and function. We concepted OOH digital experiences which I also mocked-up.

    Portrait View
    Alt Landscape View

    Event Tracking
    Outdoor Installation Demo

    XD Thinking

    While designing a complete mobile experience for individuals we also understand that additional markets have unique needs we need to tailor to. Our interfaces have features specific to the needs that will be needed by their respective users. The event installations give people new to Vis the opportunity to see it in action and interact with the UI over a large touch screen. The initial commercial application suggested is sports, but it can be extended into many industries not limited to wellness, military, etc.
